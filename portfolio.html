[ Publications ]

A Hybrid Feature Engineering and Supervised Learning Approach for Anomaly Detection in HDFS Logs

Published: August 17, 2025

Abstract: This project introduces a novel hybrid methodology that analyzes HDFS block IDs in conjunction with their log message context to achieve state-of-the-art anomaly detection. By engineering over 60 features and using a tuned XGBoost classifier, the model achieved 0.9998 accuracy and an F1-Score of 0.9961 on the Logpai dataset, proving its suitability for high-stakes environments.
